REQ-ID: NFR24
Description: The product shall ensure that a patient is only authorised to view details of their own appointments.
Type: 15 (Security)
Rationale: To ensure the confidentiality of patient related information is not compromised by unauthorised users as this could lead to the clinic being held criminally responsible for a breach of the data protection act.
Fit Criterion: An authenticated patient must only be able to view their appointments in accordance with section 7.8 (Access control) of BS EN ISO 27799:2008
